Victor Matheus de Castro Geraldo Castro

I am Victor Matheus (also known as Geraldo Castro), 26, I am from Mossoró/RN but today I live in Natal/RN. I am backend developer at Evolux. I also am an organizer of the Grupy-RN community with three more friends of mine. I usually give lectures about my areas of interest and acting in open and/or free events. I interrupted the baccalaureate of Science and Technology at the Federal University of Rio Grande do Norte to focus on programming and start my career. I started this year (2019) a major in Computer Science at Estácio de Sá University. During the time I studied engineering, I excelled in subjects that involved programming.

Some of my work experiences have been in various areas such as juridical, hospital, gaming, IoT, teaching, data capture and processing, modeling and project management. Nowadays, one of my principles is to pass on all my acquired knowledge to others and also learn from them. In my free time I like to study human behavior and their relationships with others and with the technologies, and I usually look for open source projects and try to contribute in some way.

Projects

Jararaca Project

It's a open source system initially created to manage Grupy-RN events and automate tasks related to it. Created using Python (Django) and JavaScript (React), the application runs in a web browser (desktop or mobile).
Open Source.

RestSMSGateway

REST SMS Gateway allows to change your phone into a powerful SMS Gateway which you can control from a computer. This library aims to improve the usability of the API.
My first library published in Pypi.
Open Source.

Simple Web Crawler

Project to search for links in web pages recursively. The intention was to apply some prior knowledge to a new idea. Using Docker, MongoDB and Python, the script can capture only internal links, as well as "mine" links from other domains and capture the entire internet (or almost).
Open Source.
